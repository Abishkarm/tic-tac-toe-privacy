Privacy Policy

App: Tic Tac Toe – All in One

Last updated: May 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Tic Tac Toe – All in One does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details.

2. Permissions

This app does not use the microphone, camera, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device permissions. The only permission used is INTERNET, which is required for the online multiplayer feature.

3. Data Storage

Game settings and scores are stored locally on your device only using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not shared with us or any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
